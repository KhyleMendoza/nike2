ABOUT

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation that is dedicated to the design, development, manufacturing, and global marketing and sales of a wide range of high-quality footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. Headquartered near Beaverton, Oregon, in the Portland metropolitan area, Nike has established itself as the leading supplier of athletic shoes and apparel worldwide. In its fiscal year 2022, the company generated a staggering revenue exceeding US$46 billion.

Renowned for its excellence, Nike specializes in creating innovative and stylish products for athletes and sports enthusiasts alike. With its iconic "swoosh" logo, Nike has become a universally recognized symbol of athletic performance and style. The company's extensive product portfolio encompasses various sports and activities, reflecting its commitment to catering to the diverse needs and preferences of its customers.

At Nike, we are committed to expanding the world of sports. With over fifty years of experience and a steadfast dedication to listening to athletes, we continue to push the boundaries of innovation. Our products and services are designed to be inclusive, considering not only the physical aspects but also the mental and lifestyle aspects of individuals. We strive to empower athletes and inspire a culture of active living.
